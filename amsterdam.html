FAQ

Are there extra measurements because of COVID-19 (the Coronavirus)?

  1. It is mandatory to wear a face mask inside the museum.
  2. It is mandatory to present a negative PCR test, a vaccination passport or proof of recovery to get into the museum.
  3. Keep a minimum of1,5 meters distancefrom other visitors
  4. Visiting the museum? Online booking is needed – buy Tickets here.
  5. Our cloakroom is temporary closed.

I have booked a ticket on a partner site without a time slot. How can I book a time slot?

Click here and select partner ticket and choose your date and time slot. Please, bring both tickets with you to the museum and they’ll be scanned at the entrance.

I have an I Amsterdam Card. How can I book a time slot?

Click here and select a stadspas and choose your date and time slot. Please, bring the ticket and your valid pass to the museum, where they’ll be scanned at the entranc
